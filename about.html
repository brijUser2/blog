About
Mike Trivedi founded his software company - Trivedi Solutions - in Valley Forge, PA in the early '90s.

Since then he has provided expert consulting services to many companies, including about a dozen S&P500 companies, as well as members of the DOW30, FTSE50 and CAC40.

He has also built a large number of custom apps.
One of his apps achieved an IBM ServerProvenTM designation and was sold to EU clients.

In 2018 Mike became the solo bootstrapped founder of Brij, a noCode Database System: think AirTable but with real Database capabilities, including:

  • Table/Schema Design
  • Form Design
  • DataView Design
  • Infinite Capacity
Sometime during the COVID lockdown, Mike ported Brij (then about 40k loc) from Java to FSharp. Since then he has been a Functional Programming (FP) enthusiast and advocate.
